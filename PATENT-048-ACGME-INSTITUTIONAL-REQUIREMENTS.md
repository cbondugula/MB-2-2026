## **TECHNICAL FIELD**

This invention relates to artificial intelligence systems for healthcare accreditation compliance, specifically automated systems for monitoring, verifying, and ensuring compliance with Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME) Institutional Requirements across healthcare institutions sponsoring graduate medical education programs.

---

## **BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION**

### **Current Problem**
The ACGME accredits over 650 institutions sponsoring 9,000+ graduate medical education programs. Each institution must maintain continuous compliance with comprehensive institutional requirements including:

- Designated Institutional Official (DIO) oversight responsibilities
- Graduate Medical Education Committee (GMEC) governance and operations
- Learning and working environment standards
- Institutional resource requirements
- Patient safety and quality improvement mandates
- Annual institutional review processes

**Current State**: 100% manual compliance verification requiring:
- Months of preparation for institutional reviews
- Expensive compliance consultants ($200K-$500K annually)
- High risk of accreditation violations due to oversight gaps
- Inefficient document management and evidence collection
- Reactive compliance approach vs proactive monitoring

**Market Opportunity**: Zero existing AI-powered solutions for ACGME institutional compliance automation.

---

## **SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION**

The present invention provides an AI-powered system that automatically monitors, verifies, and ensures continuous compliance with ACGME Institutional Requirements. The system uses machine learning models trained on ACGME standards to provide real-time compliance monitoring, predictive violation detection, and automated evidence generation for institutional accreditation.

### **Key Innovations**
1. **Automated ACGME Compliance Verification**: ML-based analysis of institutional policies and procedures
2. **Real-time Monitoring Dashboard**: Continuous tracking of all institutional requirements
3. **Predictive Violation Detection**: AI algorithms that predict compliance risks before they occur
4. **Automated Evidence Generation**: AI-powered documentation and report creation for ACGME reviews
5. **DIO Oversight Automation**: Intelligent assistance for Designated Institutional Official responsibilities

## **PATENT CLAIMS**

### **Claim 1** (Primary Independent Claim)
A computer-implemented system for automated compliance monitoring and verification of ACGME Institutional Requirements, comprising:
- a) A knowledge base containing comprehensive ACGME Institutional Requirements and compliance standards
- b) A real-time data collection module that continuously monitors institutional systems and processes
- c) A machine learning compliance verification engine that automatically assesses adherence to ACGME standards
- d) A predictive analytics system that identifies potential compliance violations before they occur
- e) An automated reporting module that generates ACGME-compliant documentation and evidence packages

### **Claim 2** (DIO Oversight Automation)
The system of claim 1, further comprising an automated DIO oversight module that:
- Tracks and monitors all Designated Institutional Official responsibilities as defined in ACGME requirements
- Provides intelligent scheduling and task management for DIO obligations
- Automatically generates DIO performance reports and compliance summaries
- Integrates with GMEC governance systems for comprehensive institutional oversight

### **Claim 3** (GMEC Compliance Tracking)
The system of claim 1, further comprising a GMEC compliance tracking platform that:
- Automatically monitors Graduate Medical Education Committee meeting requirements
- Verifies resident/fellow representation and participation in GMEC activities
- Analyzes meeting minutes for compliance with ACGME content requirements
- Tracks action item completion and institutional policy implementation

### **Claim 4** (Real-Time Compliance Dashboard)
The system of claim 1, further comprising a real-time compliance monitoring dashboard that:
- Displays current compliance status across all ACGME Institutional Requirements
- Provides visual indicators for compliance levels and violation risks
- Enables drill-down analysis of specific institutional requirements
- Generates automated alerts for immediate attention items

### **Claim 5** (Predictive Violation Detection)
The system of claim 1, wherein the predictive analytics system employs machine learning algorithms to:
- Analyze historical compliance patterns and institutional performance data
- Identify leading indicators of potential ACGME violations
- Generate risk scores for different categories of institutional requirements
- Recommend proactive interventions to prevent compliance violations

### **Claim 6** (Automated Evidence Collection)
The system of claim 1, further comprising an automated evidence collection module that:
- Continuously gathers documentation supporting ACGME compliance
- Organizes evidence according to ACGME institutional review standards
- Generates comprehensive evidence packages for site visits and reviews
- Maintains audit trails and version control for all compliance documentation

### **Claim 7** (Integration with ACGME Systems)
The system of claim 1, further comprising integration capabilities that:
- Interface with ACGME Accreditation Data System (ADS) for automated data submission
- Synchronize with ACGME Cloud platform for enhanced functionality
- Enable automated completion of ACGME institutional annual updates
- Facilitate seamless communication with ACGME review processes

### **Claim 8** (Learning Environment Monitoring)
The system of claim 1, further comprising a learning environment monitoring module that:
- Continuously assesses compliance with ACGME learning and working environment standards
- Monitors resident/fellow well-being indicators and work hour compliance
- Evaluates patient safety and quality improvement program effectiveness
- Generates automated recommendations for learning environment optimization

### **Claim 9** (Institutional Resource Verification)
The system of claim 1, further comprising an automated resource verification system that:
- Monitors compliance with ACGME institutional resource requirements
- Tracks adequacy of administrative, educational, and clinical resources
- Verifies support levels for program directors, faculty, and coordinators
- Assesses technology infrastructure and educational facility compliance

### **Claim 10** (Annual Institutional Review Automation)
The system of claim 1, further comprising an automated Annual Institutional Review (AIR) system that:
- Continuously collects and analyzes institutional performance indicators
- Automatically generates AIR reports with compliance summaries
- Tracks action plan implementation and performance monitoring
- Provides executive summaries for institutional governing body review

### **Claim 11** (Special Review Process Automation)
The system of claim 1, further comprising a Special Review automation module that:
- Automatically identifies underperforming programs requiring special review
- Generates timely reports describing quality improvement goals and corrective actions
- Tracks GMEC monitoring of outcomes and implementation timelines
- Provides early intervention recommendations for program improvement

### **Claim 12** (Multi-Institution Analytics)
The system of claim 1, further comprising multi-institutional analytics capabilities that:
- Enable benchmarking across multiple ACGME-accredited institutions
- Provide comparative analysis of compliance performance
- Identify best practices and successful compliance strategies
- Generate industry-wide trends and improvement recommendations
