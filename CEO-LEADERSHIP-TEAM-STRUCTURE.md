## **CORE EXECUTIVE TEAM (5 KEY LEADERS)**

### **1. Chief Technology Officer (CTO)**
**Hire Profile**: Former Snowflake, Databricks, or OpenAI engineering leader
- **Compensation**: $400K salary + 2-3% equity
- **Role**: Technical vision, AI platform architecture, voice technology development
- **Team Size**: 15-20 engineers by Month 12
- **Key Deliverables**: 
  - Voice-controlled development platform
  - Patent implementation and protection
  - Healthcare AI and compliance systems
- **Success Metrics**: Platform reliability, patent demonstrations, AI accuracy

### **2. Chief Operating Officer (COO)**
**Hire Profile**: Former Stripe, Airbnb, or Palantir operations leader
- **Compensation**: $350K salary + 1.5-2% equity
- **Role**: Global operations, process optimization, scaling execution
- **Team Size**: 10-15 operations staff by Month 12
- **Key Deliverables**:
  - Global operational infrastructure
  - Regional market entry execution
  - Customer success and support systems
- **Success Metrics**: Operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, global expansion

### **3. Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)**
**Hire Profile**: Former Salesforce, Oracle, or Microsoft enterprise sales leader
- **Compensation**: $350K salary + 1.5-2% equity + commission structure
- **Role**: Enterprise sales, revenue growth, strategic partnerships
- **Team Size**: 12-18 sales professionals by Month 12
- **Key Deliverables**:
  - $100M+ ARR by Month 18
  - Fortune 500 customer acquisition
  - Oracle and Microsoft partnership execution
- **Success Metrics**: Revenue growth, deal size, customer acquisition cost

### **4. Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)**
**Hire Profile**: Former OpenAI, Microsoft, or enterprise software marketing leader
- **Compensation**: $300K salary + 1-1.5% equity
- **Role**: Brand positioning, demand generation, thought leadership
- **Team Size**: 8-12 marketing professionals by Month 12
- **Key Deliverables**:
  - AI development category creation
  - Developer community building
  - $500M+ brand value establishment
- **Success Metrics**: Brand recognition, lead generation, developer adoption

### **5. Chief Financial Officer (CFO)**
**Hire Profile**: Former unicorn CFO with IPO experience (Snowflake, Databricks type)
- **Compensation**: $350K salary + 1.5-2% equity
- **Role**: Financial strategy, Series B preparation, investor relations
- **Team Size**: 5-8 finance professionals by Month 12
- **Key Deliverables**:
  - Series B fundraising ($500M-$1B)
  - Financial operations and compliance
  - Strategic acquisition analysis
- **Success Metrics**: Financial efficiency, Series B valuation, investor confidence
