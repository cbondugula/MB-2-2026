**Key Facts**:
- **US Provisional Patents**: Only provide priority date for US filing
- **No International Protection**: Provisional patents are US-only applications
- **12-Month Deadline**: Must file non-provisional OR PCT within 12 months
- **Priority Benefit**: International filings can claim priority back to US provisional date

### **Correct International Filing Timeline**

**Option 1: Direct PCT Filing (Recommended)**
- **Timeline**: File PCT within 12 months of provisional filing
- **Coverage**: International protection in designated countries
- **Priority**: Claims priority back to original provisional filing date

**Option 2: Individual Country Filing**
- **Timeline**: File in each country within 12 months of provisional
