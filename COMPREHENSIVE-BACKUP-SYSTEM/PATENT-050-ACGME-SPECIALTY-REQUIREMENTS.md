 BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION

 Current Problem
The ACGME accredits programs across 130+ distinct medical specialties, each with unique specialty-specific Program Requirements beyond the Common Program Requirements. These include:

- Specialty-Specific Clinical Experience Requirements: Unique case volumes, procedure minimums, and clinical rotations for each specialty
- Specialty-Specific Competency Milestones: Tailored milestone frameworks reflecting specialty practice patterns
- Specialty-Specific Faculty Requirements: Board certification, clinical experience, and teaching qualifications specific to each specialty
- Specialty-Specific Assessment Methods: Specialty-appropriate evaluation tools and assessment frequencies
- Specialty-Specific Research and Scholarly Activity: Requirements varying by specialty focus and academic expectations

Current State: Manual interpretation and compliance verification requiring:
- Specialty-specific expertise for accurate requirement interpretation
- Hundreds of hours annually per program for specialty compliance documentation
- High risk of citation due to misunderstanding of specialty-specific nuances
- Inconsistent application of specialty requirements across similar programs
- Limited benchmarking capabilities across specialty programs

Market Opportunity: Zero existing AI-powered solutions providing deep specialty expertise for ACGME specialty-specific requirements automation.
