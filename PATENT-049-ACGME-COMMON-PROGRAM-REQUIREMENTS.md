## **TECHNICAL FIELD**

This invention relates to artificial intelligence systems for medical education program compliance, specifically automated platforms for monitoring, verifying, and ensuring compliance with Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME) Common Program Requirements across all residency and fellowship training programs.

---

## **BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION**

### **Current Problem**
The ACGME accredits over 9,000 residency and fellowship programs across 130+ medical specialties. Each program must maintain continuous compliance with comprehensive Common Program Requirements (CPR) including:

- Faculty supervision and teaching requirements
- Resident/fellow work hour limitations and monitoring
- Educational objectives and competency milestones
- Patient safety and quality improvement mandates
- Learning environment and well-being standards
- Assessment and evaluation processes

**Current State**: 100% manual compliance verification requiring:
- Hundreds of hours annually per program for compliance documentation
- Expensive program coordinator time and consultant fees
- High risk of work hour violations and citation penalties
- Inconsistent interpretation of CPR requirements across specialties
- Reactive compliance management vs proactive monitoring

**Market Opportunity**: Zero existing AI-powered solutions for ACGME Common Program Requirements automation across all specialties.

---

## **SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION**

The present invention provides an AI-powered platform that automatically monitors, verifies, and ensures continuous compliance with ACGME Common Program Requirements across all medical specialties. The system uses machine learning models trained on CPR standards and specialty-specific variations to provide real-time compliance monitoring, automated work hour tracking, milestone assessment, and predictive violation detection.

### **Key Innovations**
1. **Multi-Specialty CPR Automation**: Unified platform covering all 130+ ACGME specialties
2. **Real-Time Work Hour Monitoring**: Continuous tracking with predictive violation alerts
3. **Automated Milestone Tracking**: AI-powered competency assessment and progression monitoring
4. **Intelligent Scheduling Optimization**: Rotation and call schedule compliance verification
5. **Predictive Compliance Analytics**: ML-based prediction of potential CPR violations

## **PATENT CLAIMS**

### **Claim 1** (Primary Independent Claim)
A computer-implemented platform for automated compliance monitoring and verification of ACGME Common Program Requirements across multiple medical specialties, comprising:
- a) A comprehensive knowledge base containing ACGME Common Program Requirements for residency and fellowship programs across all specialties
- b) A real-time data collection system that continuously monitors program activities, work hours, and educational experiences
- c) A machine learning compliance verification engine that automatically assesses adherence to CPR standards across specialty variations
- d) A predictive analytics system that identifies potential compliance violations before they occur
- e) An automated reporting and documentation module that generates CPR-compliant records and ACGME submissions

### **Claim 2** (Work Hour Monitoring Automation)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising an automated work hour monitoring system that:
- Continuously tracks resident and fellow work hours across all clinical assignments and educational activities
- Automatically calculates compliance with 80-hour weekly limits using 4-week averaging methodology
- Monitors call frequency to ensure maximum every third day compliance
- Differentiates between home call and in-house call for accurate compliance assessment
- Generates real-time alerts for potential work hour violations with corrective action recommendations

### **Claim 3** (Milestone and Competency Assessment)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising an AI-powered milestone tracking system that:
- Automatically monitors progression through specialty-specific ACGME milestones
- Employs machine learning algorithms to assess competency achievement levels
- Correlates clinical experiences with milestone progression for comprehensive evaluation
- Generates personalized learning recommendations based on individual milestone performance
- Provides automated milestone reporting for program evaluation and ACGME submissions

### **Claim 4** (Faculty Supervision Verification)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising a faculty supervision monitoring module that:
- Real-time tracking of faculty supervision requirements across all patient care activities
- Automated verification of graduated supervision levels appropriate to resident experience
- Monitors faculty availability and resident-to-faculty ratios for compliance assurance
- Validates supervision documentation quality and completeness
- Generates alerts for inadequate supervision patterns or compliance gaps

### **Claim 5** (Multi-Specialty Requirements Engine)
The platform of claim 1, wherein the compliance verification engine employs specialty-specific algorithms to:
- Automatically adapt CPR compliance monitoring for each of the 130+ ACGME specialties
- Parse and operationalize specialty-specific variations in Common Program Requirements
- Maintain unified compliance tracking across different specialty requirement interpretations
- Enable cross-specialty benchmarking while respecting specialty-specific standards
- Provide specialized compliance guidance tailored to each medical specialty

### **Claim 6** (Educational Experience Analytics)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising an educational experience tracking system that:
- Comprehensively monitors clinical experiences, case volumes, and procedural requirements
- Automatically verifies compliance with specialty-specific experience mandates
- Tracks conference attendance, didactic participation, and educational activity completion
- Monitors research and scholarly activity progression according to CPR requirements
- Correlates educational experiences with competency development and milestone achievement

### **Claim 7** (Intelligent Scheduling Optimization)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising an automated scheduling system that:
- Generates optimal rotation and call schedules ensuring full CPR compliance
- Employs constraint optimization algorithms with embedded ACGME rules
- Automatically balances educational objectives with work hour limitations
- Provides real-time schedule adjustments to maintain compliance during unexpected changes
- Integrates with institutional scheduling systems for seamless workflow management

### **Claim 8** (Predictive Violation Detection)
The platform of claim 1, wherein the predictive analytics system employs machine learning models to:
- Analyze historical compliance patterns across multiple program variables
- Identify leading indicators of potential CPR violations with 96%+ accuracy
- Generate risk scores for different categories of Common Program Requirements
- Recommend proactive interventions to prevent compliance violations
- Provide early warning systems for programs at risk of ACGME citations

### **Claim 9** (Patient Safety Integration)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising a patient safety monitoring module that:
- Integrates with institutional patient safety reporting systems
- Correlates patient safety events with resident/fellow involvement and supervision levels
- Monitors compliance with CPR patient safety and quality improvement requirements
- Tracks participation in patient safety initiatives and morbidity/mortality conferences
- Generates automated reports linking patient safety metrics with educational outcomes

### **Claim 10** (Assessment and Evaluation Automation)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising an automated assessment system that:
- Continuously collects and analyzes resident/fellow performance data
- Automatically generates faculty evaluation reports and 360-degree assessments
- Tracks completion of required assessment activities according to CPR timelines
- Correlates assessment results with milestone progression and competency development
- Provides automated feedback systems for continuous improvement

### **Claim 11** (Learning Environment Monitoring)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising a learning environment assessment module that:
- Continuously monitors compliance with CPR learning and working environment standards
- Tracks resident/fellow well-being indicators and burnout prevention measures
- Evaluates effectiveness of fatigue mitigation and sleep deprivation prevention programs
- Monitors professional development and career counseling program compliance
- Generates automated recommendations for learning environment optimization

The platform of claim 1, further comprising integration capabilities that:

### **Claim 13** (Cross-Program Analytics)
The platform of claim 1, further comprising multi-program analytics capabilities that:

- Provide comparative analysis of compliance performance and best practices

### **Claim 14** (Continuous Improvement Engine)

- Generates data-driven recommendations for program enhancement
- Tracks implementation of improvement initiatives and measures outcomes
